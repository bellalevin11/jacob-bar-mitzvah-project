Jacob's Bar Mitzvah Project

In just four months, I will have the honor of becoming a Bar Mitzvah, and I look forward with great excitement to sharing this celebration with you. I am writing to ask for your support with my Bar Mitzvah project, an endeavor I hope will help not only fulfill my social obligation but also positively impact on our community and humanity as a whole. Please allow me to share with you the journey that led me to choose Company A of Battalion 7056 in the Northern Paratroopers Brigade of the IDF as the recipients of my efforts.

As we all know too well, our beloved Jewish State has endured tremendous struggles and hardships over the past year and a half. On the morning of October 7, 2023, the terrorist organization Hamas launched a brutal attack on Israel, resulting in one of the most devastating events the Jews have experienced since the Holocaust. Hamas seeks not only the annihilation of Israel but also the extinction of Jews worldwide. Thanks to the courageous men and women of the IDF as well as Jews and non-Jews globally who uphold the values of justice and freedom, Israel is winning this costly war on all seven fronts.

The combat soldiers of Company A have been stationed along Israelâ€™s northern border for 250 days, fighting bravely and tirelessly against the threat of Hezbollah to ensure the safety and security of all civilians. Through a special project supported by my parents in collaboration with a local Israeli woman who provides homemade nutritious food to the IDF soldiers, I was able to create a personal connection with Master Sergeant Ariel Bik, commander of the company. During a FaceTime call, I asked how I could support their mission. He replied immediately and with deep gratitude that they were in urgent need of a specialized trailer to transport military equipment over long distances. Without hesitation, I pledged my full commitment to helping Sergeant Bik and his soldiers obtain the means to secure this critical piece of equipment.

As I enter a new chapter in my Jewish life, I am devoted to embracing the responsibility of contributing to the well-being of our community. My desire for others to contribute to this meaningful cause has now surpassed my natural inclination to indulge in Bar Mitzvah gifts. Therefore, I ask you to please not bring any gifts for my Bar Mitzvah, as it will not serve any purpose towards fulfilling my Bar Mitzvah project. If you can and wish to contribute to my Bar Mitzvah project, I can assure you that your donation will bring me happiness and a sense of accomplishment knowing that you helped me make a difference in this world.
